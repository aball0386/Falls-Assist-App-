Assessment Summary

AMTS - Abbreviated Mental Test Score

  1. Age: "What is your age?"
  2. Time: "What is the time to the nearest hour?"
  3. Address: Provide an address for the patient to repeat, ask the patient to recall it at the end of the test. For example, "42 West Street"
  4. Year: "What is the Current year?"
  5. Place: "What is the name of this place?" or "What is your house number?"
  6. Person Recognition: "Can you recognize these two people?" (presenting pictures or pointing out individuals like a family member or carer)
  7. Date of Birth: "What is your date of birth?" (day and month are sufficient)
  8. World War I: "In what year did World War I begin?" ans: 1914
  9. Monarch: "Who is the current monarch?"
  10. Counting Backwards: "Please count backwards from 20 to 1."
